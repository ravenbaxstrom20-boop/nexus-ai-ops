# Nexus AI Ops - UI/UX Design Document

## Vision
Nexus AI Ops centralizes fragmented business processes. The UI must be professional, intuitive, and provide a sense of "command and control" over complex operations.

## Design Principles

1.  **Contextual Intelligence:** Surfacing the right information at the right time. AI-driven alerts should be prominent but not intrusive.
2.  **Density & Focus:** High information density for power users, but structured with clear visual hierarchy to prevent overwhelm.
3.  **Modular Dashboard:** A "Command Center" that aggregates the most critical data from all modules (CRM, Jobs, Leads).
4.  **Responsive & Fast:** Near-instant transitions and a layout that adapts to the user's focus.

## Visual Language

*   **Colors:**
    *   **Primary:** Indigo-900 (#1e1b4b) - Base for navigation and headers.
    *   **Secondary:** Blue-600 (#2563eb) - Primary actions and brand highlights.
    *   **Accent:** Emerald-500 (#10b981) - Success states and "active" indicators.
    *   **Background:** Slate-50 (#f8fafc) - Light grey background for the app area.
*   **Typography:** Inter (System font stack fallback) - Focus on readability and clean hierarchy.
*   **Components:** 
    *   Rounded corners: `0.5rem` (8px).
    *   Elevation: Subtle shadows (`shadow-sm` to `shadow-md`) to separate cards from the background.

## Core Views

### 1. Command Center (The Dashboard)
The landing page. It should contain:
*   **Active Job Stats:** Total jobs, overdue tasks, upcoming deadlines.
*   **Lead Pipeline:** A snapshot of the sales funnel.
*   **Recent Activity:** A feed of automated actions and manual updates.
*   **AI Recommendations:** "Smart" tasks generated by the workflow engine.

### 2. CRM / Lead Management
A table/list view with powerful filtering. Each record shows history, current status, and linked jobs.

### 3. Job Tracking
A Kanban board for visualizing the flow of delivery.

### 4. Workflow Monitor
A view to see active automations, their success rates, and any errors.

## Tech Stack (Frontend)
*   **Framework:** React (Vite)
*   **Language:** TypeScript
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
*   **Icons:** Lucide React
*   **State Management:** TanStack Query (React Query) for server state.
